This is reflection section on learning

As a beginner in this field, designing a portfolio webpage from scratch is something I had never thought but I am very happy because this project gave me the opportunity to experience learning an unknown field. I was very overwhelmed at the beginning and had no idea where to start, so I started to re-watch the ilecture recording for revision and then looked for information online.


I was intrigued that although my initial code and test runs failed, the challenge of troubleshooting the problem motivated me to keep trying. It was a time-consuming process, but I felt an indescribable sense of satisfaction once the issue was resolved. Despite this, I feel that this page falls short of my vision, as I am still limited in my ability to take it to the next level. However, with more practice, I hope to be able to bridge the gap between my ideas and the reality of my web pages.
