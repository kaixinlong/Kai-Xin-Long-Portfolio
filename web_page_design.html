Web Page Design

 

The Idea

 

Okay, so you have an idea for a website or an assignment due for Business Web Technologies.  You want to make it look professional and easy to use.

 

What is the first thing you need to work on?

 

Content

 

Content is the most important element of a website.  Written content, makes up for more than 90% of the Web. 

 

The aim of design is to enhance the presentation of the content!  Styling content will go a long way.
